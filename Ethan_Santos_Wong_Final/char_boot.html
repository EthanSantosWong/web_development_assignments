The Faces of Eathur

Eathur has three races: humans, elves, and dwarves; as well as the cross breeds. There are no real differences other than phyical ones. Elves have pointed ears and taller, dwarves are a little more squat and tend to have more luxourious facial hair; even the the females, and humans are average all around.

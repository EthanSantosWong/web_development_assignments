Eathur: a world where magic and old timey tech come together.

Greetings, my name is Ethan. Welcome to a world of my creation.

Eathur is a world of Steampunk Fantasy, basically it is a high fantasy setting that has begun to develop steam-age technologhy.

With the combination of magical crystal that can control the elements the citizens of Eathur have been able to come up with fantastic flying machines, powerful engines and a host of other crazy contraptions.

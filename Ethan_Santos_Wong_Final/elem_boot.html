The Source of Power in Eathur

Elemental crystals are the crux of almost everything within Eathur. They power machines, houses, vehicles and a lot of other things. Most major settlements in Eathur are made around major deposits of crystals becasue they provide that much power.

As stated before these elements can harness the power of the elements: being fire, water, wind, and stone. However there have been reports of being crystals of other elements, but those are rare naturally. Thus, scientists have tried, and succeeded, in imbuing elements such as electricity into used up crystals. How and why these crystals can give off and take in these elemental energy is still under heavy research.

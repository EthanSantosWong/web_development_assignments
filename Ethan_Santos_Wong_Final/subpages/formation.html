Where does the Power Come From?

The most accepted theory on how elemental crystals form is linked to large natural phenomenon, such as earthquakes, forest fire or floods. Scientists postulate this is because all natural things: fire, water, air, etc. Give off a unique elemental energy and when there is a lot of one type of elemental energy in one place it somehow solidifies into a crystaline structure. Now whether or not elemental crystals form because of large natural events or these natural events occur due to a crystal form is currently under rigiorous study.

The biggest piece of evendence behind this theory is the Nivallese Empire. Fire crystals did not form in that area until it separated from the main land, and evidently a majority of the amount of crystals form within the Nivallese capital castle whiched turned into an active volcanic site. Of course there are many other hints around Eathur, such as the humongous earth crystal deposut within Mt. Kareem, or the deep maratine trench underneath Oowan.

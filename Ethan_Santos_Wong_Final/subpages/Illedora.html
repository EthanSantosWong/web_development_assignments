The Elvish Rouge

Illedora Dahlalsi (the main protagonist) is part of an asssotiation of rough spies that have opperated in Eathur since the olden days. The reason whe is in the assosiation is because it is family tradition, her mother and both her grandparents on her mother's side were a part of it and now Illedora has taken up the mantle.

However she is not alone, when Illedora was young she met Swetella "Sweeny" CeanKeal, her eventual rouge partner and girlfriend. Illedora also has the help of three civilian imformants: Shellma Zellwinslow, Zorag Ria and Rantonio Shenjingu. A traveling acrobatics trope that was saved by Illedora and Sweeny, from then on the three of them swore to help the two rouges with whatever they needed.

Illedora lives with Sweeny within their flying home with a land deed in Kinetail. This way the two of them can easlity respond to any mission the association gives them. As well as allow the couple to freely travel to any location they fancy.

Illedora grew up in the middle class teir of Steammor Metropolis where she would study during the school year. During the Summersshe would frequently be sent a rouge training camp within the Shadowshine Forest to the north of Kinetail. This camp was where she met Sweeny. Illedora would go on to major in imbuing elements into hollow crystals at Glemstein Academy in Steammor, where she would use the knowlege of how to imbue elements to enhance her arsenal as well as her rouge gadgets.

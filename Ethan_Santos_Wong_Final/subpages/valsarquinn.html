A City among the Clouds

Val Sarquinn is the newest settlement to arise within Eathur, about fifty years old or so. There is not as elabrate a backstory for such a grand city as the others. Bascially, a group of graduates for Glemstein Academy in Steammor saw the rise of flying houses technology and stated: "I bet we can make a city with this." We can see the result of that

The first thing that the team of ambitious urban planners was to find air crystals; and after a lengthy exhibition they found and excavated a large crystal formation within a cave in the south eastern region of Eathur. Next the team had to create a sturdy and suitable base to carry an entire metropolitian area on top of it. To put it simply it required a lot of time and outside help.

Currently Val Sarquinn is the leader in both medicne resaerch as well as some of the arts in Eathur. Due to Val Sarquinn being quite new this attracted quite a number of young people fresh from collage.

An Empire with a Fiery Past
Long ago Nivall was a thriving empire within the eastern region of Eathur. However, 200 years ago they decided they, for some unknown reason, decied to go completely isolationist and seperated their empire from the Eathur. Quite literally.

Historical records state that the king of Nivall at that time, Elphoranous Cazamine, preformed great feat of magic by raising up the Cazamine Mountain Range. Followed by him phyically pushing the Nivallese Empire's land eastward from the main land of Eathur, which in turn created the Cazamine Canyon. We know now that that "magic" was actually just earth elemental crystals.

Nivall stayed in its isolationist state until a hundred years ago. Within those hundred years the climate and ecosystem of Nivall had completely changed. The spawling green fields had turned into dried up drought lands due to the mountain range blocking incoming moisture from the west. To top that off, due to the mass shift of the techtonic plates a lava vein had formed under the capitol of Nivall. As well as turning the location of the castle within the capitol into a volcano!

However, this turn of events is not all bad. With the lava vein many fire crystals naturally formed in and around the Nivall castle; and these fire crystals have proved lucrative for the empire during the early stages of this steam powered age.

A Variety of Intresting Cities in Eathur
Eathur has a wide range of intriguing locals to vist. From the grand tiered city of Steammor Metropolis. Or relax at the high class and accomadating resort archepelego of Oowan off the west coast. Or maybe one would like to gain a bird's eye view of everything from the flying city of Val Sarquinn.

However if the the urban life is not one's style, the village of Kinetail is set near a lush forest in front of the great Zoundpin Mountain Range. Although there have been some rumors of a monk village high in that range, so people who want a more spiritual expirence should get ready for an adventure.

The final two locations that are of note are a tad more grittier, in a cleanliness sort of fashion. The Municpality of Elmon is a mining and major trading post on the face of Mt. Kareem to the south. Meanwhile the Nivallese Empire is a place where history has gone wrong; a once great city situated amongst rolling hills and sprawling hills brought to fire and sand, all due to arrogace and isolationism.

Recipe: Tater Tot Cassarole

Ingreidents

  • 1 Lb of ground beef
  • 1 can of Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • 1 bag of tater tots
  • 1 half onion

Procedure

  • Preheat oven to desired temperature on tator tot package
  • dice half onion
  • cook onion and ground beef until brown
  • add cream of mushroom and one can of water to meat
  • let simmer of a few minutes
  • (Optional) take out excess soup to prevent cassarole from being to wet
  • place soup and meat micture into a container
  • place tater tots over the mixture
  • bake for 50 minutes, or until tater tots are sufficently cooked/brown
  • enjoy tater tot cassarole.... (or don't it is up to you)
